About
Established in 1996, LUST is a graphic design practice in the Netherlands. It was established by Jeroen Barendse, Thomas Castro, and Dimitri Nieuwenhuizen. The company does a wide variety of work from printwork and book design to data-visualizations to interactive installations and architectural graphics. The company is successful in creating work that is easy to understand. Their data-visualizations make the information easy to understand as well as fun to go through. Lustlab, created in 2010, researches, generates hypotheses and makes unstable media stable again. They have formed a platform where knowledge, issues and ideologies can be referred to.
